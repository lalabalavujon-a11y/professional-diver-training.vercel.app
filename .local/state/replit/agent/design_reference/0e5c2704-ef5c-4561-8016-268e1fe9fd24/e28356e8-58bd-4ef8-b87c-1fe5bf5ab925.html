Understanding Respiratory Mechanics

The human respiratory system is a complex network of organs and tissues that work together to facilitate gas exchange. For divers, understanding these fundamentals is crucial for safe diving practices.

Key Components

  • Lungs: Primary organs for gas exchange
  • Diaphragm: Main muscle for breathing
  • Airways: Pathway for air movement
  • Alveoli: Sites of gas exchange

Breathing Process

During inspiration, the diaphragm contracts and moves downward, creating negative pressure that draws air into the lungs. Expiration typically occurs passively as the diaphragm relaxes.
